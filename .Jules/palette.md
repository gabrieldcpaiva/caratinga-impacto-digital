## 2024-05-24 - Explicit Form Label Associations
**Learning:** Many input fields in the application had descriptive text near them but lacked explicit `<label for="...">` associations, which breaks screen reader support and reduces the clickable area for users.
**Action:** Always ensure that every `<input>` has a corresponding `<label>` with a matching `for` attribute to improve accessibility and usability across all forms and calculators.
