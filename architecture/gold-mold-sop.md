# SOP: The Golden Mold (Prototipo de Luxo)

## 1. Objective
Create a scalable, high-conversion landing page and proposal template for local businesses in Caratinga.

## 2. Design DNA (CARE Inspired)
- **Theme:** Light (Cream/Paper).
- **Typography:** 
    - Headings: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Bold).
    - Accents/Italics: `Cormorant Garamond` (Elegant).
    - Body: `Inter` or `Plus Jakarta Sans`.
- **Palette:**
    - Background: `#F2F0E9` (Cream).
    - Primary: `#2E4036` (Moss Green).
    - Accent: `#CC5833` (Clay Orange).
    - Text: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal).
- **Atmosphere:** Noise overlay, rounded corners (32px), generous whitespace.

## 3. Structure (Mobile-First)
### Landing Page (index.html)
- **Hero:** Authority headline + "Alcance Perdido" hook.
- **The Gap:** Visualizing what the business is missing (Reverse FOMO).
- **The Instrument:** Interactive Calculator (ROI or Lost Opportunity).
- **The Service:** Atomic breakdown of the GMB/Positioning solution.
- **CTA:** Seamless WhatsApp transition.

### Proposal (proposta.html)
- **Certificate of Value:** Instead of a generic list, a structured "Value Audit".
- **The Problem:** Local demand search volume data.
- **The Solution:** Visual mockups of the new presence.
- **The ROI:** Calculated impact on their bottom line.
- **The Close:** Direct "Secure Your Spot" button.

## 4. Technical Stack
- Vanilla HTML5 / CSS3.
- Minimal JS (for calculator and scroll animations).
- No Tailwind CDN (to avoid integrity issues), use localized minimal CSS or the "Void" base refined.

## 5. Reverse FOMO Triggers
- "Seu concorrente está a 1 clique de distância."
- "X pessoas pesquisaram por [Serviço] em Caratinga hoje. Quantas te acharam?"
